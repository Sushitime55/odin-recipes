Fried Chicken

The juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken of all time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Four pounds of chicken wings, legs, and/or thighs
  • Buttermilk: Acidic buttermilk to tenderize the chicken
  • Seasonings: Paprika, salt, pepper
  • Flour: Gives buttermilk and seasonings something to stick to while ensuring a wonderfully crispy crust
  • Oil: Vegetable oil for frying chicken

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, paprika, salt,and pepper to make breading
  2. Place buttermilk in a bowl, then completely soak chicken in buttermilk
  3. Coat chicken in breading
  4. Pour oil in skillet and bring to high heat
  5. Brown chicken on both sides, then reduce heat and cover skillet for 30 minutes
  6. Remove cover, return heat to high, then fry until extra crispy
