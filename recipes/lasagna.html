World's Best Lasagna

Originally created by John Chandler, can be customized to your liking!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage, lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: One onion, two cloves garlic
  • Tomatoes: One can crushed tomatoes, two cans tomato sauce, two cans tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodes: store-bought or homemade
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • Egg: One egg

Instructions

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna according to recipe instructions (see below)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining parmesan
