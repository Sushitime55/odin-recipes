Beef Pho

Vietnamese pho with tangly rice noodles, thinly sliced beef, and a variety of spices

Ingredients

  • Broth Flavoring: Beef bones and tough beef cuts, oxtail optional
  • Salt, sugar, fish sauce
  • Aromatics and spices: Ginger, green onion, clove, star anise
  • Flat rice noodles
  • Steak: Tender cut, sliced thin

Instructions

  1. Clean bones and oxtail with salt and cold water
  2. Fill stock pot with water and let boil
  3. Simmer bones and oxtail in pot
  4. Add spices to broth as needed
  5. Add noodles and thinly sliced steak to empy bowl
  6. Ladle bowl with beef broth
  7. Add condiments, herbs to finish flavor and enjoy
